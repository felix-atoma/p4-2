📚 French Definite Articles: THE

Understanding "THE" in French

In French, "the" has three forms depending on the noun:

LE - Masculine

Used before masculine nouns

le lit - the bed

le salon - the living room

le fauteuil - the armchair

LA - Feminine

Used before feminine nouns

la chambre - the bedroom

la cuisine - the kitchen

la table - the table

L' - Before Vowels

Used before nouns starting with a vowel (a, e, i, o, u)

l'armoire - the wardrobe

l'oreiller - the pillow

l'assiette - the plate

💡 Remember!

You need to learn whether each noun is masculine or feminine. There's no easy rule, so it's best to memorize the article with the noun!
